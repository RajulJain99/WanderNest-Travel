Maldives

About Maldives

Maldives is a tropical paradise in the Indian Ocean, known for crystal-clear waters, overwater villas, and coral reefs. It’s the ultimate destination for honeymooners and beach lovers.

Key Highlights

  • Best Time to Visit: November to April
  • Weather: Warm and sunny with little rain
  • Top Attractions: Snorkeling, diving spots, luxury resorts, Male city
  • Local Cuisine: Mas Huni, Garudhiya, Coconut-based curries
  • Fun Tip: Try a night-time snorkeling tour under the bioluminescent waters
