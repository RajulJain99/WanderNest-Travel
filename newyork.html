New York

About New York

New York City, the city that never sleeps, is a global hub of culture, fashion, finance, and entertainment. From Times Square to Central Park, it offers an electrifying mix of iconic landmarks and diverse neighborhoods.

Key Highlights

  • Best Time to Visit: April to June & September to November
  • Weather: Cold winters, warm summers
  • Top Attractions: Statue of Liberty, Broadway, Central Park, Empire State Building
  • Local Cuisine: New York pizza, bagels, cheesecake
  • Fun Tip: Catch the skyline at night from Brooklyn Bridge
