About London

London, the capital of the United Kingdom, is a historic and modern metropolis known for its iconic landmarks, diverse culture, and global influence. From Buckingham Palace to the London Eye, it offers a perfect blend of tradition and trend.

Key Highlights

  • Best Time to Visit: March to May
  • Weather: Mild with frequent rain
  • Top Attractions: Big Ben, Tower Bridge, British Museum, Hyde Park
  • Local Cuisine: Fish and chips, English breakfast, Afternoon tea
  • Fun Tip: Take a ride on the London Eye during sunset for amazing views
