Singapore

About Singapore

Singapore is a vibrant city-state known for its cleanliness, modern skyline, and cultural blend. From futuristic gardens to bustling hawker centers, it offers a perfect mix of nature and technology.

Key Highlights

  • Best Time to Visit: February to April
  • Weather: Hot and humid throughout the year
  • Top Attractions: Marina Bay Sands, Gardens by the Bay, Sentosa Island, Orchard Road
  • Local Cuisine: Hainanese Chicken Rice, Laksa, Chili Crab
  • Fun Tip: Visit Jewel Changi Airport, even if you're not flying!
