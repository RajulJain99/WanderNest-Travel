Tokyo

About Tokyo

Tokyo, Japan’s capital, is a futuristic city that blends tradition with cutting-edge tech. From ancient temples to anime culture, it’s a place where the past and future coexist beautifully.

Key Highlights

  • Best Time to Visit: March to May & October to November
  • Weather: Varies with four distinct seasons
  • Top Attractions: Shibuya Crossing, Tokyo Tower, Senso-ji Temple, Akihabara
  • Local Cuisine: Sushi, Ramen, Tempura, Mochi
  • Fun Tip: Visit during cherry blossom season for magical scenery
