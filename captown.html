Cape Town

About Cape Town

Cape Town, nestled between mountains and the sea, is one of South Africa’s most stunning cities. It boasts rich history, breathtaking landscapes, and vibrant markets.

Key Highlights

  • Best Time to Visit: March to May & September to November
  • Weather: Mild and dry with cooler winters
  • Top Attractions: Table Mountain, Robben Island, V&A Waterfront, Boulders Beach
  • Local Cuisine: Bobotie, Biltong, Braai
  • Fun Tip: Hike or take the cable car to the top of Table Mountain for sunset
