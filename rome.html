Rome

About Rome

Rome, the capital of Italy, is a city where ancient history and modern life coexist beautifully. Known as the "Eternal City," Rome offers awe-inspiring ruins, Renaissance art, and vibrant street culture.

Key Highlights

  • Best Time to Visit: April to June & September to October
  • Weather: Warm and sunny in spring and fall
  • Top Attractions: Colosseum, Vatican City, Trevi Fountain, Roman Forum
  • Local Cuisine: Pasta Carbonara, Gelato, Pizza al Taglio
  • Fun Tip: Throw a coin in the Trevi Fountain to ensure your return to Rome
