Goa

About Goa

Goa, located on the western coast of India, is known for its stunning beaches, vibrant nightlife, and Portuguese-influenced architecture. It's a perfect getaway for beach lovers, partygoers, and culture enthusiasts alike.

Key Highlights

  • Best Time to Visit: November to February
  • Weather: Tropical with warm sunshine
  • Top Attractions: Baga Beach, Fort Aguada, Dudhsagar Falls, Anjuna Flea Market
  • Local Cuisine: Goan fish curry, Bebinca, Vindaloo
  • Fun Tip: Rent a scooter to explore the coastal villages freely
